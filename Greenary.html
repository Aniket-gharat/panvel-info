~~ The Greenary of the Panvel ~~


- Greenery in our living environment benefits more than just our health and well-being. It also facilitates water management and promotes biodiversity in built-up areas, and can help reduce the effects of noise pollution. Greenery also helps to raise the property value of homes and offices.

- Access to nature has also been found to improve sleep and reduce stress, increase happiness and reduce negative emotions, promote positive social interactions and even help generate a sense of meaning to life.

- Plants are more than just decoration. They make our lives brighter simply by being in a room with us, on our window sills, or in balcony gardens. Research shows that because we have such a strong positive association with plants, their absence in our lives can actually cause physical and mental stress.



Karnala Bird Sanctuary


- TIf you are a nature lover, this bird sanctuary will be the best Panvel tourist place for you for its admirable assortment of exotic plants and over 200 species of native & migratory birds. You can choose from interesting trails like Hariyal and Mortaka nature trails to sight birds like Malabar Lark, Small Sunbird, Nilgiri Woodpigeon, Slaty Legged Crake, etc. Over 30 varieties of migratory birds like Bluethroat, Ashy Minivet, and Red-breasted Flycatcher visit the sanctuary each year.

-Don’t miss to see the colourful butterflies as you walk amidst the beautiful wilderness. If you visit during the monsoons, you can see many small ravines bubbling over with water accentuating the beauty of the sanctuary much more. The sanctuary also houses the ancient 12th-century Karnala Fort, which is a must-visit.
Location: Karnala Village, Raigad District, Maharashtra.
Best time: July- August, and October- February.
Timings: Every day 7:00 am to 5:00 pm
Price: Entry fee per person: Rs. 35, Camera charges: Rs. 100



SMART Eco Park


- SMART eco Park is a one of a kind 20 acre eco-park that aims to teach children skills they will need in the next 50 years through an interactive learning experience. Please note that visits to the park are by prior booking only. We currently accept bookings from schools and private groups.

- Smart is a non- profit world class eco park that aims to teach children vital skills which they will need the most in the next 50 years such as the ability to be a good global citizen, to be at peace with each other and within and to be sustainable/ eco friendly. These skills are taught using interactive, immersive cutting edge methods developed by international experts in a 20 acre studio provided by nature.
Location: Smart Eco Park Age Group: 3+
Meeting Point & time: Smart Eco Park at 9:30 am
Trip duration: 7 hours



Vadale Lake


- The centuries-old lake and precincts are spread over 25 acres, adjoining Mumbai-Pune Highway. The location is 1.5km from Panvel station, 1km from Panvel bus depot and 800 meters from PCMC headquarters. The lake had lost its charm due to poor upkeep which was once known for abundance of flora and fauna like lotus flower and habitat of migratory birds.

- After completion of the major revamp and beautification work of Panvel’s oldest Vadale Lake , also known as Ballaleshwar Lake, which was built by the Peshwas, Panvel City Municipal Corporation (PCMC) has decided to illuminate the lake periphery through colourful and design LED lights soon.
- Meanwhile, the civic administration plans to spend an estimated Rs 5.23 crore for the lake illumination project. A tender has been floated inviting expert bidders to carry out the lighting work around the lake which is visited by a large chunk of local residents daily as a major key recreational hub in the township.
Location: vadale lake



CIDCO Garden



-The garden was inaugurated by state irrigation minister and Raigad district guardian minister Sunil Tatkare in the presence of Cidco's chairman Nakul Patil and staff members. Also present on the occasion were the parents of the martyr Fight Lieutenant Tushar Shyamrao Chavan, after whom the ultra-modern garden has been named.
- The garden has the facilities of a sprawling green lawn, an amphitheatre, which can accommodate around 200 and can be used for social events and entertainment programmes. Apart from a jogging track, the added attractions are a colourful fountain, a laughter club for senior citizens and a Yoga Center.
- There are play toys for children of various age groups, like climbing walls and skating rings. For adults, there are comfortable seating arrangements and a colossal podium for organising get-togethers and cultural programmes. The garden was already planted with saplings of various species, like palm and tamarind, apart from rose bushes and very attractive and flowering trees, like gulmohar and hibiscus.
- This Garden have plans to plant rare species of trees for the botanical study for school and college students pursuing science. The garden shall now be maintained by the contractor appointed by Cidco through a tender process.""Kamble added, ""The garden shall be kept open for public from 5 am to 9 am and also from 5 pm to 9 pm. For school children to visit the garden, the timing shall be between 11 am and 4 pm.
Location: CIDCO Garden
