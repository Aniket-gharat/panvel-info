1.Shivganga Waterpark



-It`s a waterpark so the fun here is limited to those who loves to get drenched. I guess many suits that profile, especially kids. It`s a place with spending a day for two reasons (a) they`ve clean rooms, a little less spacious but managed properly and a huge garden/lawn outside. So its a break from routine and time well spent with family as well as friends. (b) If you`re adventurous then the place is sure going to make you sweat. There are many water games and slides over here. It easily takes away one half day in enjoying everything the waterpark has to offer and next half demands peaceful resting time. Food tastes average and they`ve limited menu. Gulp down whatever`s on plate and sleep tight.

2.Visava Amusement Park Karnala


- The Visava Water Park Karnala! This amazing attraction offers an exciting and relaxing day out with your family and friends. With a wide variety of thrilling rides, you are sure to have a great time. The park also has numerous food vendors, beautiful scenery, and comfortable amenities to ensure you have a comfortable and enjoyable visit. Book your tickets to the Visava Water Park Karnala and spend a picturesque day relaxing by the cool waters!
* Glide on the thrilling water slide and have an adrenalin-pumping experience at the Visava Waterpark Karnala. Bask in the sun as you float around the lazy river, a great way to unwind and relax.
* Feel as if you are on the beach without leaving the city as you enjoy a refreshing swim at the wave pool attraction.
* Kids can spend a fun-filled time in the children's play area, where they can splash around in the shallow pools and have fun on the mini-slides.
* Enjoy delicious snacks and meals from the food court, offering a variety of cuisines to suit everyone's taste buds.
Activity Location: Mumbai - Goa Highway, Near Karnala Bird Sanctuary Kalhe Gaon, Panvel Raigad, Maharashtra - 410221
Waterpark timings: 9 Am to 5 Pm
Activity timings: 9 Am to 1 Pm & 2:30 Pm to 4:30 Pm
